Loan Shark

A simple javascript mortgage loan calculator.

Use this to quickly hash out the math and total cost of a simple loan repayment plan. Input the total, the months, and finally the interest rate to get back a repayment table and total expected cost of your loan.

  • Built in javascript
  • Blazing fast.
  • Simple to use.
